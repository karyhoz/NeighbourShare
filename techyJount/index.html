Frequently Asked
Questions

Find answers to common questions about how NeighborShare works.

How do i request an item?

Simply browse the available items and click on the 'Request' button to get in touch with the owner.

Is there a cost to use NeighborShare?

No. NeighborShare is completely free to use for all community members.

How do i post an item?

Go to the 'Post an item' page, fill out the form with the item details, and submit it to share with community.
